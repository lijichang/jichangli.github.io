Short Bio

I am a Ph.D student at Department of Computer Science, the University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Yizhou YU.

My research interests are Computer Vision and Deep Learning, mainly including Semi-supervised Learning and Domain Adaptation.

In 2020, I received my Master's Degree of Engineering in Computer Science at South China University of Technology, advised by Prof. Zhiwen YU and Dr. Si WU. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's Degree of Engineering at South China Normal University in 2017.
