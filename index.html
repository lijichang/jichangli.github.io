Short Bio

I am a Ph.D student at the Department of Computer Science, the University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Yizhou YU (IEEE Fellow). I also work with Prof. Guanbin LI from Sun Yat-sen University.

My research interests are Computer Vision and Deep Learning, mainly focusing on weakly supervised learning such as Semi-supervised Learning, Domain Adaptation and Learning from Noisy Labels.

In 2020, I received my Master's Degree of Engineering in Computer Science at South China University of Technology, advised by Prof. Zhiwen YU and Prof. Si WU.
